    About Hospital Finder

    Hospital Finder is a web application designed to help users quickly locate hospitals, clinics, and medical centers within a 5km radius of their current location. Utilizing modern web technologies, the application leverages geolocation to pinpoint the user's position and displays nearby healthcare facilities on an interactive map. The application provides detailed information for each facility, including name, type, address, contact information, services offered, operating hours, and user ratings. This tool is particularly useful for individuals seeking immediate medical assistance or simply exploring healthcare options in their vicinity. Hospital Finder is responsive and user-friendly, ensuring a seamless experience across various devices, including smartphones, tablets, and desktops.
